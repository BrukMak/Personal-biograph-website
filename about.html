About Me

A software engineer with a diverse set of skills and experiences. With expertise in programming languages such as Python, C++, Java, and Dart, I've worked on a range of projects, from mobile app development to web development. In addition to my technical skills, I'm also experienced in database management, having worked with MongoDB, Ms SQL, and MySQL. I'm committed to ensuring that the data I work with is secure, accurate, and accessible, and I use my expertise in data structure and algorithms to optimize performance and efficiency. As a UI/UX designer, I'm proficient in Figma, and I use this tool to create intuitive and visually appealing designs that enhance the user experience. I'm also an instructor at A2SV, where I teach data structure and algorithms to students who are interested in pursuing a career in software engineering.
